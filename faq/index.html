It depends on your company’s needs. We do the same work which your own full time CFO or Controller does. But we have more experience and expertise and we do it at affordable fees /costs.

Question

Answer

Apart from doing work online or on phone “Excellent CFO Support” also works physically onsite at the client’s location where we can be most effective. 

Question
Why do I need a Expert CFO Support if I have a Accountant?

Answer

Excellent CFO Support are professionals in financial management. That is all we do. Accountants are professionals in financial statement preparation, and auditing. Asking your Accoutant to assist your company in financial management and strategic planning is a little like asking your architect to build your building.

Question
Why do I need a “Excellent CFO Support” if my company has a Financial Controller?

Answer

You probably do not need “Excellent CFO Support “ if your Financial Controller  provides you with timely, accurate, and insightful financial and operational information about your business; then he is the person you trust to help you create and update your strategic plans; also can help you assess your organizational efficiency and is helping you lead your company to the next level. He has assembled a talented and professional accounting staff and can put together a financial package, effectively present it to your banker, and get the financing you need. If your Financial Controller cannot do these things, a “Excellent CFO Support” can.
