OUR SERVICES

Management Services

We normally work at a senior level giving expert guidance to the owners and departmental heads. Owners of small companies generally feel that they are very lonely at the top. They frequently don’t have anyone with whom they can discuss strategies and issues. Your CFO support is just as a good sounding board for the owners. Like an online partner. We have a wealth of experience, which a company owners can tap tap into:

Strategic planning assistance
We work with your staff to identify and quantify the important factors that will affect your bottom line & future growth and success. We use that information to prepare financial projections and plans and providing you narratives/findings that will help you visualize the future of your company.
Growth plan development and management
Operating a growing business without a growth plan can be risky. The likelihood of success is much higher if there is a plan for when the business needs to add cash, people, training, knowledge, capacity, distribution channels, and more. We can help you create your plan and follow it.
Financial & cash flow projections development
We will take of your vision of your company’s future and turn it into a meaningful financial projection. It will tell you if you should expect to have excess cash or there will be shortfall of cash & whether you will be requiring cash. If you require cash to grow, we will prepare cash flow projections, which will show your bank your position so they can analyze and lend money. It will also help you plan when you will need to hire more people and add more equipment and/or facilities. Normally financial projections are part of the answers for a business owner for the majority of the problems which they face in the various business scenarios.
Budgetary control, planning and implementation assistance
Whether you need us to develop a budget procedure, modify your existing procedure, or you just want assistance in it. Also do you require our help in making a budget for the year that makes sense, we will help you to formulate a budget that is based on your company’s past results and your future vision.
M I S - Finacial and opertional results analysis & management
We design well laid out proper Management Information System – which helps proper financial & operational analysis for decision making and action taking.
Profitability improvements
We will assess your operation looking for policies or procedures inefficiencies that may be costing your company money. We will look for excesses in people or materials or other misallocations of resources. We can set up systems to help you measure productivity on an ongoing basis.
Key success factors
All businesses have key factors. If we monitor this key factors properly it gives an indication of how the business is doing. Key indicators can tell you what is happening today and can sometimes predict what will happen in the future. We may find key factors in production, distribution, overtime statistics, sales operations, order taking, cash collections, inventory levels, backlog statistics, or other areas depending on the type of business you are in. We can identify these factors and set up a process for tracking and analyzing them.
Information systems assessments
We will assess your existing systems to make certain they are operating efficiently and providing accurate results on a timely basis. Where systems are inadequate, or are non-existent, we can develop them for you. This might include, among others, your financial accounting system, cost accounting system, purchasing/accounts payable process, operations process flow, or system for measuring operations performance.
Risk and opportunity assessment
We will look at your operation in the context of your industry and the general business environment. We will use our 20+ years experience and business insight to help you identify those risks and opportunities that may affect your company in the near and long term. We can also help you plan how you can avoid the risks and take advantage of the opportunities.
Organizational structure & financial staff assessment and recommendations
As businesses grow and change, their organizational structure frequently evolves into one that is less than optimum. The business may have become one that has misallocations of resources. It may not function like the well-oiled machine it once was. We can look at your business objectively, help you visualize your optimum structure, and create a plan to implement the changes that you decide you want to make. Also we can manage the staff members onsite which may allow the business owners to focus on other aspects of the business rather than spending time managing the accounting function. We will identify staff members who are in the wrong job, and we can help find suitable replacements. We also recommend incentive plans for them.
