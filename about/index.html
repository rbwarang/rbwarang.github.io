ABOUT US:

R.B. Warang C.A. formed “Excellent CFO Support “in 2013 with the idea that Large sized companies are not the only companies that can benefit from experienced financial professionals. He recognized that the impact of a single, critical decision can be more significant for a smaller and medium sized company than for a larger one. Excellent CFO Support” fills the role of a CFO for smaller and medium sized companies, giving them a higher level of financial management expertise so that they are not required to hire any high paid CFO on a full-time basis.

R.B. Warang has set a high standard for the level of expertise, skill and experience. His 30+ years of experience began with 12 years experience in a medium sized Group in Gulf. In the early years he could use his unique combination of expertise & experiences to work in the Head Office of Group of companies. .He got an opportunity to learn about many different unit companies in many different industries. He learned primarily to be a business and commercial person and then to be an accounts & finance person .That experience period helped him develop an understanding, of the factors that help a business to succeed.

There after R.B. Warang moved and joined another reputed private Group where he continued his business experience in both small and medium scaled companies. There were around 25 companies in a various broad range of industries including manufacturing, industrial processing, consumer products, and consumer services. He had been Group Finance Head (CFO) for both the Groups he worked for. This is typical of the experience and expertise you can expect from your “ Excellent CFO Support “.
